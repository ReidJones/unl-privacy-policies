A Background on Student Privacy

In February, 1969, the Supreme court ruled that students do not “shed their constitutional rights to freedom of speech or expression at the schoolhouse gate.” Since then, several pieces of legislation have been passed and several Supreme Court cases have been decided that define the rights that students do and do not have, including the scope of a student’s right to privacy.
